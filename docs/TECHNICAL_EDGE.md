# LearnTrack — Technical Edge & Value Proposition

---

## 1. LearnTrack's Unique Technical Edge (What Others Don't Have)

### Multimodal AI Quiz Generation

Generic LMS platforms force teachers to manually type out every single question and multiple-choice option. LearnTrack allows a teacher to upload heavy DepEd PDF modules or even uneditable image-based PowerPoint slides, and the system instantly outputs a structured, database-ready quiz.

### Native DepEd Grading Engine

Free platforms use standard, US-centric percentage grading. LearnTrack is hardcoded for the Philippine public school system. The moment a student finishes an AI-generated assessment, the backend instantly calculates their grade using the exact DepEd transmutation tables (Written Works, Performance Tasks, Quarterly Assessments).

### Adaptive E-Learning Logic

If a student scores 3/10 on a Google Form, the form does nothing. LearnTrack tracks the specific topics the student failed and dynamically generates targeted remedial quizzes or review modules focusing exclusively on their weak points.

### Centralized Principal Telemetry

Google Classroom traps data inside individual teacher silos. LearnTrack features a unified PostgreSQL database, allowing you to build an "Admin Dashboard." The principal gets a real-time, school-wide view of failing grades, attendance, and asynchronous engagement without asking 50 teachers to manually export spreadsheets.

## 2. The ROI of Customization (Why the School Should Pay)

When defending the cost (like that $12/month DigitalOcean VPS), you do not talk about server specs. You talk about **Return on Investment (ROI)** in terms of administrative efficiency and local compliance.

### Eliminating Unpaid Overtime

A school isn't paying for cloud hosting; they are paying to save their faculty hundreds of hours. By automating quiz creation and grade transmutation, teachers reclaim massive amounts of time that can be redirected toward actual teaching rather than manual data entry.

### Cheaper Than Paper

When you break down the micro-cost of the Gemini 2.5 API (roughly ₱0.06 per generated quiz) and a basic VPS, the annual digital infrastructure cost is drastically lower than the school's annual budget for printing physical test papers, ink, and DepEd modules.

### Total Data Ownership

With free enterprise platforms, the school is at the mercy of Google or Microsoft's ecosystem changes. By funding a custom LMS, the school retains 100% ownership of their student data and analytics, securing it on their own terms.

### Zero-Friction Localization

A customized system is built exactly for the users. Teachers do not have to "hack" or use complex third-party add-ons to make the LMS fit the DepEd curriculum; the platform was fundamentally engineered around it from day one.
